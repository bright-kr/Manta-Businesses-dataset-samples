## Data Points

| Field Name | Description |
|------------|-------------|
| `company_name` | The official name of the business entity. |
| `business_state` | The state where the business is registered or operates. |
| `sic_code` | Standard Industrial Classification code indicating the business type. |
| `phone_number` | Contact phone number for the business. |
| `website` | Official website URL of the business. |
| `business_categories` | Categories that define the business activities and industry. |
| `business_city` | City where the business is located. |
| `business_country` | Country where the business is situated. |
| `business_zip_code` | Postal code for the business location. |
| `closed_on` | Indicates if the business has closed down, if applicable. |
| `opening_hours` | The time of day when the business opens. |
| `closing_hours` | The time of day when the business closes. |
| `latitude` | Geographical latitude coordinate of the business location. |
| `longitude` | Geographical longitude coordinate of the business location. |
| `location_type` | Type of business location, e.g., retail, office. |
| `year_established` | The year the business was founded. |
| `avg_opening_hour` | Average hour of the day when the business typically opens. |
| `avg_closing_hour` | Average hour of the day when the business typically closes. |
| `business_street_name` | Street address of the business location. |
| `description` | A brief overview of the business and its operations. |
| `services` | List of services provided by the business. |
| `links` | Additional links associated with the business, if any. |
| `estimated_annual_revenue` | Approximate annual revenue generated by the business. |
| `num_employees` | Number of employees working in the business. |
| `review_count` | Total number of reviews received by the business. |
| `reviews` | Customer reviews and feedback about the business. |
| `owner` | Name of the business owner or primary contact. |
| `manta_link` | URL to the business profile on the Manta platform. |
| `url` | General URL associated with the business. |
| `company_id` | Unique identifier for the business within the dataset. |
